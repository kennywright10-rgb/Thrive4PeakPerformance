FAQs & Wellness Blog

Your questions answered and the latest in performance optimization

What should I expect during my first visit? +

Your first visit begins with a comprehensive consultation with one of our board-certified physicians. We'll review your health history, discuss your wellness goals, and perform any necessary assessments. Based on this information, we'll create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs. The entire process typically takes 45-60 minutes.

Are your therapies safe? +

Absolutely. All our therapies are overseen by board-certified physicians and performed using medical-grade equipment. We follow strict safety protocols and only offer treatments backed by scientific research. Every client undergoes a medical screening before starting any therapy to ensure it's appropriate for their health profile.

How often should I receive treatments? +

Treatment frequency varies based on your goals and the specific therapy. For example, IV drips may be recommended weekly or monthly, while cryotherapy might be beneficial 2-3 times per week for athletic recovery. During your consultation, we'll create a customized schedule that aligns with your objectives and lifestyle.

Do you accept insurance? +

Most of our wellness therapies are considered elective and are not typically covered by insurance. However, we offer competitive pricing, package deals, and membership options to make our services more accessible. We can provide detailed receipts that you can submit to your HSA/FSA or insurance company for potential reimbursement.

Can I combine multiple therapies? +

Yes! Many of our clients benefit from combining therapies for enhanced results. For example, pairing cryotherapy with red light therapy can optimize recovery, or combining IV nutrition with compression therapy can maximize athletic performance. Our physicians will recommend the best combinations for your specific goals.

Who can benefit from your services? +

Our therapies benefit a wide range of individuals, including athletes seeking performance optimization, busy professionals managing stress and fatigue, individuals recovering from injuries, people focused on anti-aging and longevity, and anyone looking to elevate their overall wellness. We customize our approach for each client regardless of their starting point.

How long do sessions typically last? +

Session lengths vary by therapy: Cryotherapy is 2-3 minutes, IV Drips are 30-60 minutes, Compression Therapy is 20-30 minutes, Red Light Therapy is 10-20 minutes, Hyperbaric Chamber sessions are 60 minutes, and HOCATT sessions are 30 minutes. We'll work with your schedule to find appointment times that fit your lifestyle.

What makes Thrive different from other wellness centers? +

Unlike typical wellness centers, every therapy at Thrive is physician-led and overseen by board-certified doctors. We use only medical-grade equipment, source our nutrients from award-winning compounding pharmacies, and base all recommendations on rigorous scientific research. Our comprehensive approach and commitment to personalized care set us apart.

Do you offer package pricing or memberships? +

Yes! We offer various package options and membership levels that provide significant savings for clients who plan to use our services regularly. Memberships include discounted rates, priority booking, and exclusive access to special promotions. Contact us to learn more about our current package and membership offerings.
